🚀 Case Studies

Bellabeat Case Study

  • Bellabeat is a high-tech company that manufactures health-focused smart products.
  • Their smart devices collect data on activity, sleep, stress, and reproductive health, empowering women with insights into their well-being.
  • This case study focuses on analyzing fitness data from smart devices to uncover new growth opportunities for Bellabeat.
  • The main subject of the study is the Bellabeat App, which provides users with data-driven insights into their daily activity, sleep patterns, stress levels, menstrual cycle, and mindfulness habits.
  • By leveraging this data, Bellabeat aims to help users make healthier lifestyle choices.
